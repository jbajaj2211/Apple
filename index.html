Fun Facts About Apple Inc.

  • Apple was founded on April 1, 1976, by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne.
  • The original Apple 1 computer was hand-built by Wozniak and sold for $666.66.
  • Apple's first logo featured Sir Isaac Newton sitting under an apple tree, designed by Ronald Wayne.
  • The famous Apple logo with a bite was designed to prevent it from being mistaken for a cherry.
  • Apple is the first company to reach a $2 trillion valuation in 2020.
  • The Apple Store was designed to look like a glass cube, creating a modern shopping experience.
  • Apple's Siri voice assistant was launched in 2011 alongside the iPhone 4S.
  • Apple's headquarters is called "Apple Park," a massive circular building often dubbed "the spaceship."
  • Steve Jobs famously co-founded Apple in his garage, which has now become a symbol of startup dreams.
  • Apple has a secret laboratory called "The Underdome" where new products are tested and developed.
