Interesting facts about Airpods Pro 3

      Airpods Pro 3 feature improved active noise cancellation, providing a better listening experience by reducing background noise.
      They incorporate Apple's new H2 chip for better performance, connectivity, and audio quality.
      The Airpods Pro 3 feature improved sweat and water resistance, making them suitable for workouts and outdoor activities.
      Apple also gives us the option to engrave our name on the Airpods 3 case.
      For the first time, AirPods Pro 3 include a heart rate sensor, allowing users to track their heart rate and calories burned during workouts without needing an Apple Watch.
